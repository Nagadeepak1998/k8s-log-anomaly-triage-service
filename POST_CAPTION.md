Built a new portfolio project: `k8s-log-anomaly-triage-service`.

It is a production-shaped FastAPI + CLI service that scores Kubernetes logs, identifies likely incident classes, returns runbook steps, exposes Prometheus metrics, and includes Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform, tests, and CI documentation.

The focus is practical platform engineering: faster incident triage, deterministic behavior, deployable service shape, and recruiter-readable evidence across DevOps, MLOps, and SRE skills.
